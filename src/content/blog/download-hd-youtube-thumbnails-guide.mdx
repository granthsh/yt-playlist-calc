Every YouTube video has a thumbnail image stored on Google's CDN at multiple resolutions. Our **YouTube Thumbnail Grabber** lets you preview and download any of these resolutions in one click — no watermark, no login, completely free.

## What Thumbnail Resolutions Exist

YouTube stores each thumbnail at four standard sizes:

| Resolution | Dimensions | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Max Resolution (MaxRes) | 1920 × 1080 | Blog hero images, social media cards, presentations |
| Standard Definition (SD) | 640 × 480 | General web use, article embeds |
| High Quality (HQ) | 480 × 360 | Default YouTube quality, forum posts |
| Medium Quality (MQ) | 320 × 180 | Thumbnail grids, low-bandwidth |

**Important:** YouTube does not serve true 4K thumbnails. Max Resolution at 1920 × 1080 (Full HD) is the highest available. Videos uploaded in standard definition or older content may lack the MaxRes variant.

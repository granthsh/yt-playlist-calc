## Why YouTube Does Not Show Total Playlist Duration

YouTube's interface is designed around individual video consumption, not batch viewing. When you open a playlist, you see:

- A list of video titles
- Individual timestamps (e.g., 12:34)
- The channel name

But nowhere does YouTube sum those timestamps into a total. This is a well-known gap that has frustrated students, professionals, and casual viewers for years. Whether you are evaluating a coding bootcamp, planning a study session, or just curious how long a playlist runs, you need a dedicated tool.

## How to Find a Playlist ID

Before calculating, you need the playlist's unique ID. Every YouTube playlist URL contains one:

- **Desktop browser:** Navigate to the playlist. The URL looks like `youtube.com/playlist?list=PL_abc123DEF`. The string after `list=` is the ID.
- **Channel page:** Click a channel's "Playlists" tab, open the one you want, and copy the address.
- **YouTube mobile app:** Open the playlist, tap Share, and copy the link. Extract the `list` parameter.
- **From a video:** Below the video player, click the playlist name to see the full URL.
